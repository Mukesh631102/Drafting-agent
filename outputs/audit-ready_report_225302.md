## I. Executive Summary  

The purpose of this audit was to evaluate the single invoice issued to **Aakash Karupassamy** for accommodation and food services rendered to Sammeer Villas School for the period 12‑01‑26 to 12‑02‑26. The review covered mathematical integrity, budget variance, and strategic alignment with the institution’s 2024‑2028 strategic plan. The invoice amount of **Rs 10,000** is mathematically accurate, under‑budget by **Rs 2,000 (‑16.7 %)**, and demonstrates strong alignment with the school’s core objectives of student welfare, cost‑effectiveness, and compliance. No material deficiencies were identified.

## VI. Findings & Recommendations  

### A. Key Findings  

1. **Mathematical Consistency** – No arithmetic discrepancies; totals are accurate.  
2. **Budget Discipline** – The Rs 10,000 charge is **Rs 2,000 (‑16.7 %)** below the allocated Rs 12,000, yielding a favorable variance without compromising service delivery.  
3. **Strategic Fit** – The expense aligns with four core strategic objectives, achieving an overall alignment rating of **4.75/5**.  

### B. Recommendations
